Maritime Networks

  • Demo for the presentation 'Random walks in dynamic maritime networks'
  • International Workshop on Maritime Networks ERC 'World Seastems'
  • Simple Demo showing the evolution of ports activity.

These graphs show the activity of ports bsed on a windowed time frame. A sliding time window of 15 days shows which ports are actually active.

The top/left panel shows year 1996 while the bottom/right panel illustrates year 2006. Each dot is a port. Active ports during the considered time windows have a brighter dot.
